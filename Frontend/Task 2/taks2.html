Grandma's Recipe Apple Pie

This apple pie has been in my family for generation and taste delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 table spoon of cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apple nicely peeled and sliced

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 375 o
  2. In a bowl mix the cinnamon and sugar
  3. Toss the apple in the sugar cinnamon mixture
